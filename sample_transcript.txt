Meeting Transcript — Q2 Product Strategy Review
Date: April 10, 2026

---

James Park: That's significant. Marcus, can we realistically scope a reporting MVP in Q2?

Diana Flores: I second that. We've had two churns this quarter where mobile experience was cited as the reason. The numbers speak for themselves.

James Park: AI-assisted search. We promised this to key accounts in our roadmap last October. We're now two quarters behind. The plan was to integrate our embedding pipeline with the search index, but we deprioritized it due to the GCP migration.

Sarah Chen: Right. We can't keep pushing this. What's the realistic timeline if we start now?

James Park: If the GCP migration wraps up by end of April as planned, we can begin the AI search integration in early May. I'd say 8 to 10 weeks to a production-ready feature with basic relevance scoring and filters.

Diana Flores: That would be transformational for our mid-market segment. I have five prospects who specifically asked about smart search in the last two weeks.

Sarah Chen: Excellent. Objective Three — ship AI-assisted search by end of June. Now let's talk action items.

Marcus Williams: I'll own the reporting MVP spec. I'll have a detailed scope document ready by Wednesday and will coordinate with Laura on the design handoff.

Laura Nguyen: I'll deliver the reporting wireframes by April 17th and share session recordings with Marcus and Ravi by end of today.

Diana Flores: I'll prepare a one-page competitive analysis on the reporting features our top three competitors offer — that should inform what we prioritize in the MVP. I'll have it ready by Tuesday.

James Park: I'll finalize the GCP migration timeline and confirm the May 1st start date for the AI search integration. I'll also open a ticket to kick off the mobile performance sprint planning.

Sarah Chen: Great. Before we close — Diana, can you reach out to the three stalled enterprise deals and let them know we're committing to a reporting MVP by end of Q2? That could help us close those contracts in the next 30 days.

Diana Flores: Absolutely, I'll send personalized updates today.

Sarah Chen: Perfect. So to summarize: we're aligning on three Q2 objectives — reporting MVP, mobile performance, and AI search. The team has clear owners and deadlines for each action item. We'll reconvene in two weeks for a mid-sprint check-in. Thanks everyone — let's make this a strong quarter.
